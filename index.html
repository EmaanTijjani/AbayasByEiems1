REFUND AND EXCHANGE POLICY

Thank you for shopping with Abayas by Eiems. We value our customers and aim to ensure satisfaction with every purchase.

EXCHANGES

We accept exchanges within 3 days of receiving your order.To be eligible for an exchange;

  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in its original condition.
  • Tags (if applicable) must still be attached.
  • The item must not be damaged, stained, altered, or washed.

Customers are responsible for exchange shipping costs unless the wrong item was sent by us.

REFUNDS

We do not offer refunds on orders once payment has been made, except in cases where:

  • You received the wrong item.
  • The item arrived damaged.

If there is an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with clear photos/videos as proof.Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Custom-made orders
  • Sale/discounted items
  • Worn or washed items

      • ORDER CANCELLATIONS

      Orders can only be cancelled within 12 hours of payment confirmation. After this period, production or processing may already have begun.

DELIVERY

At Abayas by Eiems, we are committed to ensuring your orders are delivered safely and on time.

DELIVERY LOCATIONS

We currently deliver nationwide within Nigeria and offer international shipping to selected countries.

Estimated Delivery Time

  • Lagos: 1–3 business days
  • Other states within Nigeria: 1-7business days
  • International deliveries: 7–21 business days

Please note that delivery times may vary during holidays, sales periods, or due to courier delays.

SHIPPING FEES

Delivery fees are calculated based on your location and order size. Shipping costs will be displayed before order confirmation. Customers will be notified once their order has been shipped. Tracking information will be provided once your order has been shipped (where applicable).
